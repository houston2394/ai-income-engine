# Notion Template: Ultimate Business Planner

## Template Structure

### Dashboard (Main Page)
- Welcome header with motivation quote
- Quick links to all sections
- Today's focus widget
- Progress overview (goals, projects, finances)

### Annual Goals
- 2026 Goals database
  - Goal name
  - Category (Personal, Business, Financial, Health)
  - Target date
  - Progress %
  - Status (Not Started, In Progress, Complete)
- Quarterly breakdown views
- Goal reflection templates

### Monthly Planner
- Monthly calendar view
- Focus areas for month
- Key milestones
- Habit tracker
- Monthly review template

### Weekly Review
- Week overview
- What went well
- What to improve
- Next week priorities
- Gratitude log

### Project Tracker
- Project database
  - Project name
  - Status (Backlog, In Progress, Blocked, Complete)
  - Priority (High, Medium, Low)
  - Due date
  - Related goal
  - Notes
- Kanban board view
- Timeline view

### Finance Tracker
- Income log
- Expense categories
- Monthly budget
- Savings goals
- Financial snapshot

### Templates Included
- Meeting notes
- Daily journal
- Brainstorming session
- Decision log

## Design Style

- Clean, minimal aesthetic
- Color-coded categories
- Easy to read typography
- Mobile-friendly views
