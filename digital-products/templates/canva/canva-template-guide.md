## INSTAGRAM POSTS (20 Templates)

### 1. Quote of the Day (5 variations)

**Dimensions:** 1080 x 1080px

**Design:**
- Solid background color (rotate: coral, blue, green, purple, black)
- Large centered quote text
- Small author name at bottom
- Minimal design

**Text Placeholders:**
- Quote: "Your inspiring quote here"
- Author: "Author Name"

**Variations:**
1. Coral (#FF6B6B) - Motivational
2. Blue (#4ECDC4) - Professional
3. Green (#95E1D3) - Growth/Mindset
4. Purple (#A855F7) - Creative
5. Black (#1A1A1A) - Bold
